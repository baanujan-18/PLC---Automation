# Demonstration Video

This video demonstrates the PLC-based weighted voting system using a Siemens S7-200 PLC, four voting switches, a CD4511BE BCD-to-seven-segment decoder IC, and a seven-segment display.

The demonstration shows how different shareholder voting combinations produce the corresponding weighted vote totals.
